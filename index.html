How it works

Four steps, and it's just your storage from then on.

Nothing to rent, nothing to migrate later — Stora sets up around a Discord server you already control.

1

Create your server

Spin up a private Discord server with a #storage and #manifest channel. Stora's setup wizard walks you through it.

2

Invite Pipeline

Add Stora's bot to your server with read and delete access to those two channels — nothing else.

3

Upload from Stora

Drag files into the web app. They're written to Discord as message attachments and indexed in your manifest automatically.

4

Browse, anywhere

Open Stora on any device to search, preview, star, and download — Discord just holds the bytes.

Why Stora

Everything a cloud drive does — none of the bill

No new account to pay for. You already have a Discord account — Stora just gives it a file browser.

No monthly storage fees

Stora runs on your own Discord server, so there's no per-gigabyte bill creeping up over time.

Your server, your data

Files live in a Discord server only you control — not a shared bucket on someone else's cloud.

Large files, handled quietly

Anything over 10MB is split into chunks on upload and reassembled on download — you never notice.

Real previews

First-page PDF previews and image thumbnails on hover, so you can find a file without opening it.

Isolated by design

Every user gets their own server, bot connection, and manifest — nothing is ever shared across accounts.

Bulk actions built in

Select, zip, and download or delete multiple files at once, from a list or grid view — whichever you prefer.

Where your files actually live

Your files live in a private Discord server that you create and own. Pipeline reads and writes to it the way any normal, compliant Discord bot does — nothing hidden, nothing that bends Discord's rules.

That also means your storage's uptime and availability depend on Discord, not just on Stora — the same tradeoff as any self-hosted tool. Keep a backup of anything irreplaceable, same as you would with any storage provider. Stora isn't affiliated with or endorsed by Discord.
